About Me

Turning structural plans into reality, at height.

I'm Jay Sah — a Site Engineer based in Sydney with a Master of Engineering (Structural) from UTS, specialising in the full delivery lifecycle of high-rise residential and mixed-use developments.

From deep excavation and raft foundations through to structural frames, facade systems, and internal fitout, I manage every critical stage on-site. I work closely with subcontractors, project managers, and inspectors to ensure works are delivered to the highest standards of quality, safety, and NCC compliance — without compromising programme.

My background bridges both structural theory and practical site delivery, giving me the ability to read complex engineering drawings, identify risks early, and coordinate multi-trade interfaces effectively. I thrive in fast-paced environments where precision matters.
